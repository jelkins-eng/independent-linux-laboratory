# Calibre Library Catalog: Export for NotebookLM

This document contains a curated list of books from a personal Calibre library, organized by theme. The collection heavily features topics on digital nomadism, libertarian economics, self-reliance, sailing, and classic literature.

## Theme: Digital Nomadism, Remote Work & Travel

- **The Digital Nomad Escape Plan: Expat Guide for Working Remotely in Chiang Mai, Thailand** by Michael Hulleman (2015)
  - *Tags:* travel, Asia, Thailand, telecommuting, digital nomad, geo-arbitrage.
  - *Summary:* A comprehensive guide for transitioning from a day job to a location-independent lifestyle in Chiang Mai. Covers visas, cost of living, co-working spaces, and cultural adaptation. It is a "what to do if you already make money online" guide.

- **Webworktravel - 77+ great budget destinations for Digital Nomads** by Johannes Völkner
  - *Summary:* A list of budget-friendly destinations suitable for digital nomads.

- **Neo-Nomadism** (Various authors, including Aydogdu, Larsson, Dev)
  - *Topics:* Qualitative studies on life as a digital nomad, the legal jurisdiction of nomads, and the definition of "neo-nomads."

- **Meeting the needs of the digital nomad business white paper** by Christian Meersman (HPE)
  - *Topics:* Impact of the digital economy on workforce, work styles, leadership, and enterprise mobility.

- **The Art of Non-Conformity: Set Your Own Rules, Live the Life You Want, and Change the World** by Chris Guillebeau
  - *Tags:* General, Careers, Business & Economics, Self-Help.
  - *Summary:* A guide to rejecting the standard path and designing a unique life.

- **A Better Life for Half the Price** by Tim Leffel
  - *Summary:* Strategies for living comfortably on a reduced budget, often by traveling or living in lower-cost countries.

- **How to Live in a Car, Van or RV--And Get Out of Debt, Travel and Find True Freedom** by Robert Wells
  - *Summary:* A guide to vehicle-based living and debt freedom.

- **The Rules of Travel: Think Twice Before Crossing the Rules** by Andy Graham
  - *Publisher:* Real Travelers Press
  - *Summary:* Advice on navigating travel boundaries and rules.

- **Voyaging on a Small Income** by Annie Hill
  - *Summary:* Practical advice for long-term sailing and travel on a budget.

## Theme: Libertarianism, Economics & Political Philosophy

- **An Agorist Primer** by Samuel Edward Konkin III
  - *Tags:* Economics, Libertarianism, Anarchism, Counter-Economics, Agorism, Praxeology.
  - *Summary:* An introduction to Agorism, a libertarian political philosophy advocating for a counter-economy to undermine the state.

- **For A New Liberty: The Libertarian Manifesto** by Murray N. Rothbard (2008)
  - *Summary:* A foundational text of libertarian theory advocating for the abolition of the state and the establishment of a free market society.

- **Your Next Government? From the Nation State to Stateless Nations** by Tom W. Bell
  - *Publisher:* Cambridge University Press
  - *Summary:* Explores the transition from traditional nation-states to stateless or polycentric legal systems.

- **The Fourth Revolution: The Global Race to Reinvent the State** by John Micklethwait and Adrian Wooldridge
  - *Summary:* An analysis of how the state is being reimagined in the 21st century.

- **Shadow Economies Around the World: What Did We Learn Over the Last 20 Years?** by Leandro Medina and Friedrich Schneider
  - *Tags:* Shadow economy, informal economy, MIMIC model, light intensity approach.
  - *Summary:* An IMF working paper analyzing the size and causes of informal economies globally.

- **The Conquest of Bread** by Petr Kropotkin
  - *Summary:* A classic anarchist work arguing for a society based on mutual aid and the common ownership of resources.

- **Society of the Spectacle** by Guy Debord
  - *Summary:* A critique of modern consumer society and the role of mass media in creating a "spectacle" that alienates individuals.

- **All the Presidents' Bankers: The Hidden Alliances that Drive American Power** by Nomi Prins
  - *Summary:* An investigation into the relationship between US presidents and financial institutions.

## Theme: Sailing, Boating & Maritime Adventure

- **Self-Sufficient Sailor** by Lin and Larry Pardey
  - *Summary:* A guide to long-distance cruising with minimal reliance on ports and services.

- **Cruising in Seraffyn: 25th Anniversary Edition** by Lin and Larry Pardey
  - *Summary:* The story of the Pardeys' long-term cruising life.

- **Storm Tactics: Modern Methods of Heaving-to for Survival in Extreme Conditions** by Lin and Larry Pardey
  - *Summary:* Advanced techniques for surviving extreme weather at sea.

- **Small Boat Building** by H. W. Patterson
  - *Summary:* A guide to building small boats.

- **Canoe and Boat Building: A Complete Manual for Amateurs** by William Picard Stephens
  - *Summary:* A classic manual for amateur boat builders.

- **The Joys of Yachting** by Andy Lear
  - *Summary:* A guide to the lifestyle and pleasures of yachting.

- **Tightwads on the Loose: A Seven Year Pacific Odyssey** by Wendy Hinman
  - *Summary:* The true story of a couple crossing the Pacific Ocean on a budget, living on a small boat.

- **Reflection on America's Great Loop: A Baby Boomer Couple's Year-Long Boating Odyssey** by George and Patricia Hospodar
  - *Summary:* A narrative of circumnavigating the eastern US and Canada via waterways.

- **Sealab: America's Forgotten Quest to Live and Work on the Ocean Floor** by Ben Hellwarth
  - *Summary:* The history of the US Navy's underwater habitat experiments.

- **Captain Cook's Journal During the First Voyage Round the World** by James Cook
  - *Summary:* Primary source journal of Cook's historic voyages.

## Theme: Self-Reliance, Survival & DIY

- **Do-It-Yourself Medicine** by Ragnar Benson
  - *Summary:* A controversial guide on obtaining and using antibiotics and other drugs without prescriptions.

- **Bug Out Vehicles and Shelters: Build and Outfit Your Life-Saving Escape** by Scott B. Williams
  - *Summary:* A prepper's guide to creating mobile and stationary survival shelters.

- **Eating Cheap** by Ragnar Benson
  - *Summary:* Strategies for minimizing food costs.

- **Living Well On Practically Nothing** by Edward H. Romney
  - *Summary:* A guide to frugal living and self-sufficiency.

- **Tree Crops: A Permanent Agriculture** by J. Russell Smith
  - *Summary:* An early argument for sustainable agriculture using perennial tree crops.

- **Woodworking for beginners: a manual for amateurs** by Charles Gardner Wheeler
  - *Summary:* Basic woodworking techniques.

- **Off the Grid: Live in a Van, Truck, Trailer, or Motorhome** by JJ Luna
  - *Summary:* A guide to vehicle-based living and off-grid power/water solutions.
